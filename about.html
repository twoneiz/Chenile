About Our Loves, Our Efforts

A small handmade project that grew from a simple hobby into meaningful gifts for many people.

Our Story

Our Loves, Our Efforts started with a love for craft and a desire to create something soft, comforting, and long-lasting. Instead of fresh flowers that fade quickly, we chose chenille yarn to design flowers that can be kept as a memory for years to come.

Every piece - whether it's a bouquet, keychain, flower card, or special edition cake box - is carefully assembled by hand. We pay attention to details such as colour combinations, balance, textures, and the story behind each order.

What We Believe

  • Handmade with Heart: We believe effort and sincerity are felt through handmade work.
  • Custom for You: No two people are the same - so we love adjusting colours, sizes, and designs to your preferences.
  • Lasting Gifts: Chenille flowers can be displayed, hugged, and kept as a reminder of a special moment.

How to Order

1. Browse & Get Ideas

Explore our Products page to see available flowers, colours, designs, and price ranges.

Note down any inspirations from social media or previous works that you like.

2. Contact & Customise

Reach out via WhatsApp with your preferred design, colour palette, budget, and date needed.

We'll discuss options and adjust details according to your preferences.

3. Pre-Order & Crafting

Once confirmed, we will start crafting your order carefully, especially if it is a custom bouquet or special edition set.

Please allow sufficient lead time as everything is handmade.

4. Delivery / Pick-Up

We'll arrange delivery or pick-up details depending on your location. Packaging will be done securely to protect your chenille flowers.

Thank You for Supporting Handmade Creations

Every order, big or small, helps us continue creating and improving. We truly appreciate your trust in our work.
